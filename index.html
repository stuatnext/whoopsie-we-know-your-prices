How To Use This

  1. Commercial pricing: compare NEXT.io products against SBC by buyer intent: access, visibility, relationship density, thought leadership, category ownership, and hospitality.
  2. Sales: build battlecards around named SBC assets such as lounges, food and drink areas, regulatory meetups, app/email sponsorships, awards, and leaders formats.
  3. Relationships: enrich email/domain rows with CRM ownership, company tier, current relationship status, and invite priority.
  4. Data QA: validate OCR-derived or medium-confidence pricing against the source page before using it externally.

Caveats

  • Currencies are kept in source currency. No FX conversion is applied.
  • Percentages are based on captured rows, not sales volume or revenue.
  • Stand-build pricing usually excludes floor-space rental and tax unless the source says otherwise.
  • Medium-confidence and OCR-derived prices should be checked against the original PDF page before they are quoted externally.
